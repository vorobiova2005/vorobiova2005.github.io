About Us

We combine ancient recipes and the latest technologies

Each batch of tea is created with extraordinary attention to detail and quality, using traditional methods of processing fruits, berries, steppe and forest herbs combined with innovative processes to ensure the best taste and aroma. We preserve the spirit of our heritage and enhance it with the latest technology to ensure that every cup of our tea impresses with its rich flavor and unsurpassed quality.
